2. PUIUTU CU UNDIȚA — 3 FIȘIERE SEPARATE, fiecare 512 x 512, transparent
   (Spune-i: "keep the cat the SAME size and position in all three images")
---------------------------------------------------------------------
2a) puiutu_idle:
Pixel-art character sprite, 512x512, transparent background. A small brown
tabby cat ("Puiutu", big friendly eyes) sitting in profile facing right,
calmly holding a small wooden fishing rod in its front paws, line going off to
the right. Relaxed waiting pose. Cozy 32-bit pixel art, soft outlines, warm
palette. No text, no ground, no baked-in shadow.

2b) puiutu_reel:
Same cat, same size/angle/style/transparent bg. Now actively reeling: leaning
back, paws gripping the rod which bends under tension, determined expression.
512x512.

2c) puiutu_bite:
Same cat, same size/angle/style/transparent bg. Surprised "got a bite!" pose,
ears up, eyes wide, rod tip jerking down. 512x512.

Pixel-art fishing bobber/float, 128x128, transparent background. A classic

Pixel-art water splash animation sheet, exactly 4 frames in a single horizontal
